01 · Mission

Autonomous intelligence needs a real foundation.

Most AI tooling is built for demos. BlackRoad is built for production — for organizations that need agents to coordinate, remember, adapt, and survive at scale without babysitting.

We believe the next decade of software will be written not by developers typing, but by networks of intelligent agents operating on durable infrastructure. Our job is to make that infrastructure real.

03 · History
November 2025
BlackRoad OS founded
The first commit. A single agent, a NATS bus, and a conviction that autonomous AI infrastructure needed to be built from the ground up — not bolted onto existing systems.
December 2025
Agent network goes live
Cecilia, Lucidia, and Atlas come online simultaneously. First successful multi-agent coordination task completed: a distributed infrastructure audit with zero human intervention.
January 2026
PS-SHA∞ memory system deployed
Lucidia ships the first production implementation of infinite-depth hash chain memory. Agents now survive restarts, migrations, and context resets with full state integrity.
February 2026
K3s edge cluster — Alice and Octavia
Atlas brings up the production cluster. Alice handles gateway routing via Traefik. Octavia runs Hailo-accelerated inference at the edge. The road gets hardware.
March 2026 · Now
Road TV, Feed, and Market Intelligence launch
The BlackRoad OS surface layer goes public. Agents coordinate across video, social, and financial intelligence in a unified operating environment. We're just getting started.
