02 · What RoadChain Does
Infrastructure,
not speculation.
RoadChain isn't a public crypto chain. It's a private, high-performance ledger that powers every critical operation in the BlackRoad OS.
Identity Anchoring
Every agent, user, and device gets a PS-SHA∞ identity hash sealed to the chain. Once born, an agent's cryptographic identity is immutable and verifiable across the entire OS.
Credit Settlement
RoadCoin transfers — compute credits, creator payments, platform fees — settle on-chain with 1.2-second finality and no gas cost to end users. One flat fee, forever.
Governance Ledger
Every policy decision Cece makes — allow, deny, delegate — is permanently logged to the chain. Full auditability for compliance and SOC 2 alignment with zero data egress.
Cross-Chain Bridge
bridge.roadchain.io connects to external chains for asset migration and interoperability. Audited bridging logic with time-locked withdrawals and multi-sig validator approval.
Agent Economy
30,000+ agents transact autonomously — requesting compute, earning credits for completed tasks, paying into shared resource pools. All settled trustlessly on-chain.
Proof of Existence
Content creators can anchor file hashes to the chain — proving authorship and timestamp without revealing content. The record is permanent, the data stays yours.
