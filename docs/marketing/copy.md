## Messaging Pillars

### 1. Sovereignty
Your infrastructure, your models, your data. No vendor lock-in, no cloud dependency, no permission required.

### 2. Persistence
AI that remembers. Hash-chained memory journals (PS-SHA-infinity) ensure your agents carry context across sessions, models, and providers.

### 3. Governance
Built-in compliance, audit trails, and access control. Every action is logged, every decision is traceable.

### 4. Local-First
Run on Raspberry Pis, run on bare metal, run on your own cloud. The edge is your center.

### 5. Operator-Led
One CLI to rule them all. `br` routes to 37+ tools, orchestrates 30,000 agents, and manages 17 organizations.

## Brand Voice Rules

1. **Direct, not corporate.** Say what you mean. No jargon for jargon's sake.
2. **Technical, not academic.** Show the code, show the command.
3. **Confident, not arrogant.** We built something real. Let it speak.
4. **Warm, not soft.** We care about sovereignty AND about people.
